Information Streamix Collects

We want you to understand the types of information we collect as you use our services. We collect information to provide better services to all our users—from figuring out basic stuff like which language you speak, to improving app stability.

Streamix is designed as a "privacy-first" application. We do not require account registration, and we do not collect personal identities, email addresses, or contact lists.

Information we collect is stored with unique identifiers tied to the application or device you’re using. This helps us maintain your preferences across sessions.

Third-Party Analytics

We use third-party analytics tools like Firebase to collect diagnostic data about app usage, such as crashes and general interactions. This data is used solely for improving user experience and knowing what is trending. It is not linked to any personal information like email addresses or identity.

Do we share your information?

Streamix uses various third-party SDKs to provide core functionalities (such as media playback and image loading). We do not explicitly provide any private personal information to these third-party SDKs. We ensure that the SDKs we use are compliant with global privacy laws, including GDPR.

  • Firebase Analytics by Google: Privacy Policy
  • ExoPlayer / Media3: Used for media playback.
  • Coil: Used for image loading.

We do not share your information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of Streamix, except when we have a good-faith belief that disclosure is reasonably necessary to meet applicable laws, enforce Terms of Service, or protect against harm to the rights or safety of our users.

Local Storage

Streamix stores certain information locally on your device to enhance your experience. This includes application settings, favorites, watchlists, and search history. This data remains strictly on your device and is not uploaded to our servers. You can clear this data at any time via the app settings or system settings.

Permissions Requested & Why

  • INTERNET: Required for fetching content feeds and streaming media.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to determine connection quality to optimize loading.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Required to save downloaded files to your storage (where applicable).
  • REQUEST_INSTALL_PACKAGES: Allows the app to update itself to the latest version.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to send alerts for updates or reminders.
  • WAKE_LOCK: Keeps the CPU awake during media playback or long-running tasks.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: Used for scheduled reminders and background tasks.

Keeping Your Information Secure

We use encryption to keep your data private while in transit. We review our information collection and storage practices to prevent unauthorized access to our systems.
